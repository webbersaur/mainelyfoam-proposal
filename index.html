What We Found

Why Google Isn't Sending You More Customers

Think of your website like your storefront. Right now, the lights are on but the sign is hard to read. Here's what's going on and what it means in plain English.

🔎

Google Can't Read Your Pages Properly April: Still Unresolved

Every page on a website needs a short summary (called a "meta description") that tells Google what the page is about. It's what shows up as the little blurb under your link in search results. Right now, none of your pages have one. That means Google is guessing—and it usually guesses wrong. People see a confusing blurb and scroll right past you.

📍

No Pages for the Towns You Actually Serve April: Still Unresolved

When someone in Bridgton or Conway searches for insulation, Google looks for pages that specifically mention those towns. Your site mentions Denmark, Fryeburg, and Portland in passing—but there are no dedicated pages for them. Your biggest competitor, Anderson Insulation, has 25+ pages for individual towns. That's a huge reason they show up and you don't. We'd build pages for whichever towns you want to target—you know your service area better than anyone.

💰

Nothing About Cost or Pricing April: Still Unresolved

One of the most common things people type into Google is "how much does spray foam insulation cost in Maine." It's the kind of search where someone is ready to pick up the phone. Right now, no spray foam company in Maine has a good page for this. That's a wide-open opportunity to be the one who shows up first.

🏠

New Hampshire Is Completely Untapped April: Still Unresolved

You already serve New Hampshire—Conway, Ossipee, Wolfeboro, and beyond. But your website barely mentions it. Almost none of your competitors target NH either. That's an entire state of potential customers searching online and finding nobody. We can change that.

📷

Your Images Aren't Working for You April: Still Unresolved

The photos on your site don't have descriptions attached to them (what's called "alt text"). That means when someone searches Google Images for spray foam insulation in Maine, your photos can't show up. Simple fix—big payoff.

⭐

Very Few Online Reviews April: Still Unresolved

Online reviews are one of the biggest factors in showing up on Google Maps. Right now you have zero reviews on Yelp and limited presence on other review sites. After 38 years, you've got plenty of happy customers—we just need to make it easy for them to say so online.
